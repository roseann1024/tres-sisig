How it all started

It all began with a craving for the unmistakable taste of Manila-style sisig. Despite our best efforts, we couldn't find it anywhere in Iloilo. Fueled by our passion for this beloved dish, we saw an opportunity to fill the gap and share our love for sisig with our community. And so, our journey began – from craving to creation, bringing the flavors of Manila-style sisig to Iloilo. Swing by soon!
